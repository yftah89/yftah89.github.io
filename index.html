Yftah Ziser

I am a Research Scientist at Facebook in Tel-Aviv, where I work on natural language processing solutions for E-commerce. Before that, I spent two great years at Amazon-Alexa, designing NLP solutions for community Q&A tasks. I completed my Phd at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, where I was fortunate to be advised by Roi Reichart.

My main focus is on Deep-Learning methods for dealing with the resource bottleneck, which seriously challenges the world-wide accessibility of NLP technology.
I am particularly interested in:

  • Transfer Learning
  • Domain Adaptation
  • Multilingual Learning
  • Repesentation Learning
  • NLP for E-commerce
