About me

I am a Ph.D. Student in the Natural Language Processing Group, at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, where I am fortunate to be advised by Roi Reichart.

My main focus is on deep-learning methods for dealing with the resource bottleneck, which seriously challenges the world-wide accessibility of NLP technology. I am interested in:

  • Deep Learning
  • Transfer Learning
  • Domain Adaptation
  • Repesentation Learning
Please don't hesitate to contact me if your research interests are in these or in related areas.
